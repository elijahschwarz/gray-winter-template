About This Template

This is a mobile responsive template that is easily customizable and designed for to be suitable for a portfolio, small buisness or personal website.

With responsive tables, a gallery feature and accessable fonts, it has all of the basic features a user needs to display their artwork, client projects, or family photos.

The responsive footer contains a contact form and social media buttons to help friends, family and co-workers stay in communication. The included fonts were selected for high readablity and accessability, and they scale nicely on both large and small screens.

This template has a winter-themed color palette and images associated with it. However, these colors and images are easily customizable to suit any chosen color palette.

Dedication

This template is dedicated to Angel "Ingy" Yates. She was a web developer and artist who passed away in 2009. In the early 2000s, she advertised her artwork and learning materials though creating personality tests with banner results which could be placed on a user's profile and would link back to her website. I first discoverted her web tutorials through clicking a banner for a personality quiz on a friend's Neopets profile.
